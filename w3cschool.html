ABOUT

Established in November 5th, 2016, Le Expensziv has endeavored to establish a restaurant of refinement and high standards, to be experienced by any individual interested in the country of France. Despite our humble Malaysian origins, we are devoted to bringing forth the French experience to our Malaysian countrymen and women, curious tourists, as well as any French national who might be feeling a little homesick. We do so by utilising authentic French recipes and imported ingredients, kept fresh by our latest in modern refrigerating technology. We prioritize the happiness of our customers through efficient and well-mannered service, provided by our well trained staff, who go through a thorough training course every 6 months.
